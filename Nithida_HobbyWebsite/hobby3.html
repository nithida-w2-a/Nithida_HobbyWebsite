What is Origami?

Origami is the art of paper folding. It originated in Japan and has become a creative hobby enjoyed worldwide. With just a single sheet of paper, you can make animals, flowers, decorations, and even complex geometric shapes.

My Experience

I started doing origami because I enjoy relaxing and hands-on activities. Folding paper helps me stay calm, focused, and creative. I love the moment when a simple sheet of paper transforms into something beautiful.
