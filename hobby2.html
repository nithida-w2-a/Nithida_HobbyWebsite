What Are Jigsaw Puzzles?

Jigsaw puzzles are pictures cut into many pieces that you assemble. They improve memory, concentration, and patience.

I began solving puzzles during my free time and now enjoy challenging ones. I love the feeling of finishing a big puzzle!
